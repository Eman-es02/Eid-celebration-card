Family Gathering

  1. Family gatherings are a common practice in society when it comes to a festive celebration.
  2. Everyone gathers around to enjoy food prepared by everyone and wearing new colorful clothes and enjoy the festive day.

Zakat al-Fitr

  1. Zakat is a form of charity to help reduce poverty in society and people in need by giving out food and daily necessity using the zakat donations.
  2. It is also an act to help us free from excessive desire and greed, to teach us self-discipline and honesty.
